Italian Spaghetti Bolognese

Some tasty spag bol

    Ingredients

  • A few carrots
  • a stick of celery
  • an onion
  • few slices of bacon
  • pound of ground beef
  • spot of wine, tin of tomatoes, glass of milky goodness

    • Method

  • Step 1 : Finely chop carrot, celery, onion, and bacon then fry
  • Step 2 : Throw in the beef and brown
  • Step 3 : Add the wine and reduce, then put in tomatoes and let cook for 2 hours
  • Step 4: Bosh in the milk and let simmer for an hour. You know how to make pasta to go with it.
